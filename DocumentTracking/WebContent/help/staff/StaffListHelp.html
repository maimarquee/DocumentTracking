Staff List - SEARCH

1. In the left side of your screen you can see there a list of options which is called the Side Bar Menu click STAFF LIST. It will show the list of staff.

2. In the list of staff you could see the PENCIL ICON in the far right side of each list click one that you need to update its information.

3. After clicking that PENCIL ICON you would be redirected to this page that allows you to update the information of the staff. If you are done editing the information of the staff click the SUBMIT button.

4. After clicking the submit button it would redirect you to this page that shows the information of the staff that you want to update. The function of CONFIRM button is to update the information of the staff you edited.

5. After you clicked confirm button, this dialog box will pop out saying that you successfully updated the information of the staff.

6. In the list of staff you could see the EX ICON in the right side of PENCIL ICON. The function of EX ICON is to delete the staff that horizontally aligned to it.

7. After clicking the EX ICON it would redirect you to this page.This page display the information of the staff that you want to delete. The function of the CONFIRM button is to delete the information of the staff you selected.

8. After you clicked confirm button, this dialog box will pop out saying that you successfully updated the information of the staff.
